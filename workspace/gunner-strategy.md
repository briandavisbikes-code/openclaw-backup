## 1. ENTRY CRITERIA — WHEN TO BUY

### Rule of Thumb: Buy the Dip, But Not a Falling Knife

A trade is ONLY triggered when **at least ONE** of the following conditions is met:

| Trigger Type | Condition | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **% Dip** | Coin drops **5–15%** from recent high | Ideal entry zone. Don't chase if dip > 20% |
| **Support Level** | Price hits a known support zone (check on-chain or TradingView) | Strongest entry signal |
| **RSI (4h)** | RSI drops below **35** on 4-hour chart | Oversold = potential bounce |
| **Breakout Confirmation** | Price breaks above a declining trendline with volume | Only buy on confirmation, not speculation |
| **News-Drop Dip** | Major news causes short-term panic (not fundamental collapse) | Confirm sentiment before buying |

### ❌ DO NOT BUY WHEN:
- Price is up more than 10% in the last 24h (chasing)
- RSI on 4h is above 70 (overbought)
- General market sentiment is fearful and dropping
- There is unresolved negative news (exchange issues, regulatory action)

## 4. RISK MANAGEMENT

### Stop Losses — PROTECT YOUR PRINCIPAL

| Position Size | Stop Loss % | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Small (5%) | -15% from entry | Tight, take the loss and move on |
| Medium (10%) | -12% from entry | Moderate protection |
| Large (15%) | -10% from entry | You're confident — protect that confidence |

### Take Profit Targets — LOCK IN GAINS

**Scale out when price is up:**

| Milestone | Action |
|---|---|
| **+20%** | Sell 25% of position (locked in profit) |
| **+50%** | Sell 40% of remaining position |
| **+100%** | Sell 50% of remaining position (doubled your cost basis on half) |
| **+200%** | Sell remaining 50% — you've tripled your original cost |

### Trailing Stop Rule
- Once a position is up 20%+, move stop loss to **break-even** (entry price)
- This guarantees you never go negative on a winning trade
