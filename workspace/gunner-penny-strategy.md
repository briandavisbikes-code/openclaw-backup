## 1. ENTRY CRITERIA — PENNY SPECIFICS

### Why Pennies Are Different
- Liquidity is lower = slippage risk
- Manipulation is easier = pump and dump targets
- Volatility is extreme = 30-50% swings are normal
- Fundamentals are weaker = many fail entirely

### Entry Triggers for Pennies

| Trigger Type | Condition | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **% Dip** | Coin drops **10–25%** from recent high | Pennies drop harder; wider dip range acceptable |
| **Support Level** | Price holds at a known floor (check daily chart) | Strongest signal — don't fight the floor |
| **RSI (Daily)** | RSI drops below **40** on daily chart | Oversold but not yet extreme |
| **Volume Spike** | Unusual volume spike on the dip | Could signal accumulation |
| **Catalyst Pending** | Upcoming news/listing/partnership within 2 weeks | Enter BEFORE, not after |
| **Exchange Listing** | Newly listed on Binance | Fresh liquidity, early adopters win |

### ❌ DO NOT BUY PENNY COINS WHEN:
- Price is up 20%+ in 24h (likely a pump, will dump)
- Weekly chart RSI is above 70 (overbought on all timeframes)
- There's a locked token unlock即将到来 (massive dilution incoming)
- Team/startup is anonymous (rug pull risk)
- Market sentiment is bearish and accelerating
- Social media is eerily quiet (no retail FOMO yet = no fuel)

## 4. RISK MANAGEMENT — TIGHTER STOPS

### Stop Losses for Pennies

| Position Size | Stop Loss % | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Speculative (5%) | -25% from entry | Expect volatility, give room |
| Opportunistic (7.5%) | -20% from entry | Moderate confidence |
| High Conviction (10%) | -15% from entry | Strong belief, but still protect |

### Why Tighter?
Penny coins can drop 30-50% in a day during market selloffs. Your stop needs to account for normal volatility while still protecting against catastrophic loss.

### Trailing Stop Rule
- Once a penny position is up **+50%+**, move stop to **break-even + 5%**
- This guarantees profit even if the coin dumps back

### ⚠️ Hard Rules:
- **If a penny coin drops 40% in 48h with no market-wide crash: EXIT immediately** — something is wrong
- **If a coin drops 50% total: Stop loss triggers automatically, do not hold hoping for recovery**
- **Never average down on penny coins** — that's how you catch a falling knife

## 5. EXIT STRATEGY — WHEN TO TAKE PROFIT

### Scale-Out Targets (Pennies Move FAST)

| Milestone | Action | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| **+30%** | Sell 30% of position | Take initial capital off the table |
| **+50%** | Sell 25% of remaining | Solid win, reduce exposure |
| **+100%** | Sell 40% of remaining | You've doubled your cost basis |
| **+200%** | Sell 50% of remaining | Let the rest run but secure most profits |
| **+500%** | Sell 75% of remaining | Once-in-a-cycle move — lock most of it |

### Penny-Specific Exit Triggers

Consider FULL EXIT if:
- Price hits a **3-month high** with **declining volume** (distribution phase)
- **RSI on weekly hits 85+** (extremely overbought)
- Major exchange announces investigation or delisting
- Team starts selling large amounts (check on-chain)
- Social sentiment reaches euphoric FOMO (time to leave the party)
- **Coin jumps 100%+ in under a week** — this is a pump, take profits immediately

### ❌ Do NOT Hold Through:
- A 50% drop hoping for new highs
- A "burn" or tokenomics change announcement (usually already priced in)
- Whispers of "partnerships" without official confirmation
- Team AMAs with no concrete deliverables
