# Produce Profs - Educational YouTube Channel

## Concept
Educational YouTube Shorts (2-3 min) about vegetables, fruits, and herbs.
**Format:** Narration + visuals (better than slides, clean production)
**Style:** Faceless - AI voice narration over curated/generated visuals
**Niche:** Produce education - history, health benefits, cooking tips

## Content Structure (3-Act per Video)

### Act 1: History (30-45 sec)
- Origin story
- Where it comes from
- How it spread globally
- Interesting historical facts

### Act 2: Health (45-60 sec)
- Biological benefits
- Key nutrients/compounds
- What it does for the body
- Scientific backing (cited)

### Act 3: Practical (45-60 sec)
- How to cook with it
- Easy preparation methods
- How to consume/digest
- Simple recipe ideas

---

## Visual Style
- Clean, slightly above basic
- Mix of: AI-generated images, stock footage, simple overlays
- Text callouts sparingly used
- Color palette per produce type
- No human face on camera

## Platform
**CapCut** - Script-to-Video feature
- Paste script → auto-generates visuals, transitions, music
- AI voice narration
- Output: 2-3 min video ready for review
- Free tier available

## Production Pipeline
1. **Research** - LT gathers facts on the produce
2. **Script** - Chief writes 2-3 min narration script (3 acts)
3. **Visual prompts** - 1st Class creates image prompts for enhancement
4. **Generate** - CapCut Script-to-Video with script input
5. **Review** - Commander reviews generated video
6. **Publish** - Upload to YouTube

## SEO Basics
- Title: "[Produce Name]: The Complete Guide" or "Everything You Need to Know About [Produce]"
- Description: 2-3 paragraph summary + timestamps
- Tags: Produce name + "health" + "cooking" + "nutrition" + related terms
- Thumbnail: Clean text overlay of produce name
