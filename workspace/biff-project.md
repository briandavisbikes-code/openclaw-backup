# Biff Goes Biff'ing - Animated Shorts Project

## Concept
**Biff** - A lovable everyman who travels from small town to small town (US and Mexico) in a beat-up van, trying different YouTube trends/quests at each stop. Everything goes wrong, never learns, always moves on to the next quest.

**Biscuit** - Biff's dog who breaks the fourth wall with judgmental looks when things go sideways.

**Format:** YouTube Shorts (60 seconds or less)
**Style:** Deadpan cartoon, minimalist
**Theme:** Perseverance, everyday struggles, humor, the open road

---

## Characters

### Biff
- Everyman, mid-30s
- Earnest but hapless
- Always fully committed to the worst ideas
- Hopeless but optimistic
- Wears: Cargo shorts, basic tee, baseball cap

### Biscuit
- Biff's rescue dog
- Perpetually confused, judgmental
- Breaks fourth wall when Biff's quest goes wrong
- Medium-sized, scruffy mixed breed

---

## Episode Formula
1. Biff and Biscuit roll into town
2. Discover a local trend
3. Goes all in, completely wrong
4. Things escalate
5. Unexpected outcome (or just failure)
6. Biscuit camera look
7. Roll out to the next town

---

## Episode Pipeline (10 Pilots)

### PILOT - Part 1: The Life
- Pandemic starts - everyone wears masks
- Boss sends everyone home "until further notice"
- Biff works from home, lonely, misses Biscuit during the day
- Biscuit waits by the door
- END WITH: Return to work policy announced - come back or you're fired

### PILOT - Part 2: The Road
- Biff goes back to the office
- There's an AI robot at his desk - "unaffected by pandemics"
- Biff is fired
- Goes home, sits with Biscuit
- Looks at the van parked outside (had it the whole time?)
- "Screw it, let's go"
- Hit the road
- END WITH: The quote - "We may not succeed at these trends... but we'll succeed in other ways"

**Premise:** Biff was replaced by AI after the pandemic, so he and Biscuit hit the road.

**Episode X: The News**
- Biff gets a call or sees social media
- The job has been filled - someone else is doing it
- They can't go back now
- They're stuck on the road
- But... they're okay with it

2. **Biff Tries Vanlife (Re-visited)**
- Establish Biff's mundane 9-5 life
- Biscuit waiting at home, looking out window
- Biff doom-scrolls YouTube trends, too tired to play
- Looks at Biscuit, looks at the window, looks at phone
- "Screw it, we're going on a quest"
- They leave in the van
- Voiceover: "We may not succeed at any of these trends... but we'll succeed in other ways"
   - Buys the worst van
   - Sets up wrong
   - Unexpected: Finds peace in a parking lot

2. **Biff Tries Cooking**
   - Follows recipe exactly, weird ingredients
   - Unexpected: Starts a food trend

3. **Biff Tries Burning Man**
   - Wrong supplies, doesn't get the culture
   - Unexpected: Becomes the mascot

4. **Biff Tries Homesteading**
   - Gets all the animals at once
   - Unexpected: Animals teach him something

5. **Biff Tries Cold Exposure**
   - Ice bath done wrong
   - Unexpected: Discovers a weird hack

6. **Biff Tries Foraging**
   - Eats the wrong thing
   - Unexpected: Becomes local celebrity

7. **Biff Tries Minimalism**
   - Throws away everything including essentials
   - Unexpected: Invited to speak at conference

8. **Biff Tries Pottery**
   - Makes ugliest bowl

9. **Biff Tries Budgeting**
   - $100 challenge, weird buys

10. **Biff Tries Learning Spanish**
    - Worst methods possible
    - Unexpected: Becomes fluent in something else
