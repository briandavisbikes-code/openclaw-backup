# Privacy Policy

**Effective: 2026-03-25**

## 1. Introduction

TruckPedia ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our API and services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
- Email address (required for account creation)
- Account credentials (hashed, not stored in plaintext)
- API keys (stored securely, accessible via your dashboard)

### 2.2 Usage Data
- API request counts and frequency
- IP addresses associated with API requests
- Timestamps of API interactions
- Subscription plan and billing information

### 2.3 Technical Data
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Device information
- Referring URLs

### 2.4 Cookies
We use cookies to:
- Maintain session state
- Remember preferences
- Analyze site traffic
- Manage authentication

You may disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not function properly.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Create and maintain your account
- Process billing and payments
- Provide and improve our services
- Generate usage reports and analytics
- Send service-related notifications
- Respond to support requests
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Data Sharing

### 4.1 We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

### 4.2 We may share data with:
- **Payment processors** (Stripe, PayPal) for billing
- **Service providers** (hosting, analytics) under confidentiality agreements
- **Law enforcement** when legally required
- **Business acquirers** in the event of a merger or acquisition

## 5. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with third-party data sources:

- **EPA** – Environmental protection data
- **NHTSA** – National Highway Traffic Safety Administration
- **Wikipedia** – Publicly available reference data
- **Manufacturer specifications** – Public product data

These sources have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

## 6. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account data | Until account deletion + 30 days |
| API usage logs | 90 days |
| Billing records | 7 years (legal requirement) |
| Support correspondence | 2 years |

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 Access
You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

### 7.2 Correction
You may update or correct inaccurate personal data.

### 7.3 Deletion
You may request deletion of your account and associated data. Some data may be retained as required by law.

### 7.4 GDPR Rights (EEA Users)
If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights:
- Right to data portability
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to object to processing
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

### 7.5 CCPA Rights (California Residents)
California residents have the right to:
- Know what personal data is collected
- Know whether personal data is sold or disclosed
- Say no to the sale of personal data
- Access their personal data
- Request deletion of personal data
- Not be discriminated against for exercising their rights

## 8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls and authentication
- Secure key management

No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to maintaining appropriate protections.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## 10. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States, which may have different data protection laws.
